About

CIT Motorsports

About the Club

CIT Motorsports creates a platform for students to apply classroom knowledge to real vehicle design, fabrication, testing, and competitive participation.

Our Vision

To build a technically strong and competitive motorsports culture that inspires students to innovate, lead, and push engineering boundaries.

Our Mission

To train members through hands-on motorsports projects, develop high-performance vehicles, and represent CIT with excellence in national-level events.

Milestones

Achievements

A few of the strongest results from CIT Motorsports across national student motorsports competitions and technical events.

2022-23

Formula Bharat - 4th Overall

One of the strongest achievements in the record, with CIT Motorsports securing 4th place overall in Formula Bharat.

2013-14

Best Team Professionalism Award

Awarded at Go-Kart, this marks an important early recognition of team discipline, presentation, and professional standards.

2019-20

Strong QBDC Performance

3rd in Design, 3rd in CAE Evaluation, 4th in Business Plan Presentation, 3rd in Endurance, 3rd in Fuel Economy, and 7th overall at QBDC.

2022-23

Quad Torc Technical Results

5th in Business Plan Presentation, 6th in CAE Evaluation, and 7th in Design Presentation at Quad Torc.

2018-19

QBDC Overall Performance

4th in Design, 4th in CAE Evaluation, and 13th overall, showing strong consistency in technical judging rounds.

2017-18

FFS and QBDC Highlights

2nd place in Cost Presentation and 8th place overall in FFS, along with 3rd place in Business Plan at QBDC.

2020-21 to 2021-22

Selected to Virtual Rounds

The team was selected to virtual stages in Baja SAE India and continued participation through online competition formats.
